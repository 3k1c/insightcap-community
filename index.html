InsightCAP streamlines local-first personal knowledge workflows without compromising your data privacy or forcing you to the cloud.

Capture and extract content from multiple sources (Web, Docs, YouTube, Bilibili).

Organize captured content into a structured, searchable long-term memory system.

Search, revisit, and reuse knowledge across various projects and connected conversations.

Edit AI-generated or AI-assisted text in a dedicated, structured distraction-free editor.

Export perfectly formatted edited content flexibly (TXT, Markdown, HTML, DOCX, or PDF).

Seamless integration with Telegram bot for quick on-the-go capture, reminders, and Q&A.

Local-first & Encrypted by Default

InsightCAP respects your privacy. Knowledge is stored locally by default. The local database is encrypted, and the decryption key is kept securely under your current Windows user account.

24-Word Recovery Phrase

During first-time setup, you must save your recovery phrase. If the local key is removed, or if the app is reinstalled on another device/account, this phrase is strictly required to unlock your data.

Custom knowledge folders are treated as user data and survive normal uninstalls unless explicitly chosen to be deleted.
